Digz Gardening Gloves - The Home Depot requested new gloves and patterns for their Digz Spring gardening glove line. As brand manager for Digz, I began creating this new glove line by choosing various Pantone colors, sketching patterns, choosing fabrics and creating glove diagrams. Customers also requested 3D pallet displays (created in Photoshop) for future sales promotions.
